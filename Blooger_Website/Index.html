JAGADHATRI PUJA

>

Jagaddhatri Mata is a form of Mother Durga, the importance of worshiping her is told at the beginning of Sharad Ritu. West Bengal is famous for the worship of Durga Maiya all over India. Devotees especially go to West Bengal these days to see the worship of West Bengal. Along with the worship of Nav Durga, Jagadhatri is also a special place of worship. It has originated from Tantra, it is in the form of Sattva along with Maa Kali and Durga. They are considered as the symbol of Rajas and Tamas.

WB: Jagadhatri Puja Pandal in Hooghly made from turmeric; management claims it will keep COVID-19 at bay

Hooghly (West Bengal) [India], November 14 (ANI): Puja pandal of Goddess Jagadhatri in Chandannagar has been made with 1000 kg of turmeric this year as the management claims that turmeric is used as an antibiotic that might help to keep COVID-19 at bay.
